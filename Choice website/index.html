Why Choice Ice Cream?

Because we believe everyone deserves great taste at an affordable price. At Choice Ice Cream, our goal is simple — to bring smiles through every scoop. We focus on freshness, affordability, and consistency. While others chase luxury, we deliver joy to families and local communities with honest pricing and simple, satisfying flavors. We may not claim to be the most premium brand — but we are proudly rooted in trust, care, and everyday happiness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread happiness by offering a wide variety of innovative and classic flavors, ensuring there’s a perfect choice for everyone, every time

Our Vision

To become the most loved ice cream brand that brings joy, freshness, and delightful experiences to every moment of life

Abdul Shukoor

Abdul Shukoor, the founder of Choice Ice Cream, is a self-made entrepreneur who started from zero with big dreams and strong determination. Coming from a humble background and facing many challenges along the way, he built the brand through hard work, patience, and a never-give-up attitude. Though his formal education ended in 8th standard, his passion for business and commitment to creating something meaningful led to the birth of Choice Ice Cream — a brand that is now trusted and loved by local communities. His journey is a true example of how dedication and belief can turn even the smallest idea into a lasting success.

Choco Bar

Our Choco Bar features a heart of smooth, creamy vanilla ice cream enveloped in a thick, decadent chocolate shell that cracks with a satisfying snap. It's pure, simple, and delicious.

Strawberry

Fresh, sun-ripened strawberries are blended into our classic cream and swirled with a sweet strawberry preserve.

Tender coconut

Our Tender Coconut ice cream is crafted with delicate pieces of real tender coconut, blended into a rich and exceptionally creamy base for a refreshing and authentic taste of paradise.
